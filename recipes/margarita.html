Margarita

Description

The Margarita was originally created when Dallas socialite Margarita Sames mixed her two favorite spirits, Cointreau and tequila, together with lime juice while on vacation in Acapulco back in 1948. This original Margarita recipe has stood the test of over 70 yearsâ€™ time.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 ounces silver tequila
  • ounce orange liqueur
  • 3/4 ounce freshly-squeezed lime juice
  • optional sweetener: agave nectar or simple syrup, to taste
  • ice
  • lime wedge and coarse salt for rimming the glass

Steps

Step 1

Make a salt rim on your glass.

Step 2

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice.

Step 3

Shake vigorously until well chilled.

Step 4

Strain into a Margarita glass.

Step 5

Garnish with a lime wheel.
